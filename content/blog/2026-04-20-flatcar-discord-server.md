We're happy to announce a new platform for the Flatcar community — [our new Discord server](https://discord.gg/PMYjFUsJyq)!

Whether you're a long-time Flatcar user or just getting started, Discord gives us a space to connect in real time and grow the community together.

## What you can do on Discord

- **Chat with others** – Meet fellow Flatcar users, maintainers, and contributors.
- **Join voice discussions** – Hop into voice channels for live conversations.
- **Get help and learn** – Ask questions and share knowledge.
- **Contribute and share ideas** – Discuss features, improvements, and the future of Flatcar.
- **Stay up to date** – Get the latest news and announcements about Flatcar.

## Join us

Come say hello and be part of the conversation: [Join the Flatcar Discord](https://discord.gg/PMYjFUsJyq)

We look forward to seeing you there!
